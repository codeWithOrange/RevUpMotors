Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a luxury German car manufacturer known for its blend of performance, comfort, and advanced technology. Mercedes offers a diverse lineup, from compact sedans to high-end SUVs and electric vehicles, all crafted with premium materials, innovative safety features, and a smooth, powerful driving experience. Their vehicles are favored for their high quality, elegant design, and cutting-edge features, such as the MBUX infotainment system and extensive driver-assistance options.

Jeep Grand Cherokee

The Jeep Grand Cherokee is a popular mid-size SUV known for its rugged capability, luxury features, and versatile design. It offers strong off-road capabilities alongside a comfortable and premium interior, making it ideal for both urban and off-road adventures. The Grand Cherokee comes with a choice of powerful engines and advanced features, including an advanced 4x4 system, high ground clearance, and sophisticated driver assistance technology.

price:₹80-100 lakh

Mercedes-Benz R-Class

The Mercedes-Benz R-Class is a luxury multi-purpose vehicle (MPV) that blends the space and functionality of an SUV with the luxury and comfort of a Mercedes-Benz sedan. It was produced between 2005 and 2017, and while it's no longer in production, it was known for its spacious interior, premium features, and smooth driving experience.

price:₹75-100 lakh

Audi R7

The Audi RS7 is a high-performance luxury sedan that combines cutting-edge technology, exceptional performance, and a stylish design. It is part of Audi's "RS" performance line, which is known for delivering top-tier driving experiences. The RS7 is equipped with a powerful engine, advanced suspension systems, and a range of luxury features that make it a standout in the sports sedan category

Tesla Model 3
